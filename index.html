How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own lab tests or timed trials. Instead, we comb through thousands of owner reviews across multiple retailers, art forums, and YouTube demonstrations. We look for patterns: which easels maintain their grip over months, which hardware stays tight, and which models have recurring complaints about wobbly legs or stripped wing nuts. We also cross-check professional reviews from established art publications and instructors who use gear day in, day out. We prioritise build quality over flashy features. An easel with a simple, solid mechanism that lasts years beats one with fancy tilt adjustments that break after a few uses. Our picks reflect a consensus of real-world durability, reasonable weight, and straightforward assembly, the stuff that actually matters when you’re trying to focus on your art, not fight your equipment.
