Quick answer

The US Art Supply 33″ Tabletop Easel is our top pick for its solid build, versatile adjustability, and compact design that fits any desk.

The Meeden 53″ Floor Easel is a great runner-up for those needing a freestanding option, while the Mont Marte Tabletop Easel is ideal for artists on a budget.

A good easel can transform your painting or drawing experience. It improves your posture, holds your work at the right angle, and frees up both hands, whether you’re working in watercolour, acrylic, or pencil. But the right easel for you depends on your space, the size of your canvases, and whether you prefer sitting or standing. Start by measuring your workspace: a tabletop easel works best on a sturdy desk or table and is easy to store, while a floor easel offers more height and range. Next, consider the maximum canvas size you typically use and how stable the easel needs to be. Finally, think about portability, if you paint in multiple locations, weight and folding design matter more than a heavy studio frame.
